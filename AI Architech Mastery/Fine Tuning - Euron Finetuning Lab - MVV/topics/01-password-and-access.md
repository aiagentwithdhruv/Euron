# Password & Account Access

## Password Reset
- Use "Forgot Password" on the login page
- Enter registered email and check inbox/spam for reset link
- If still blocked, raise a ticket with screenshot + registered email

## Access Issues
- Confirm correct login account
- Check My Learning / My Courses
- Verify payment confirmations in email
- Check course validity and purchase dates
- Support can merge workspace/profile accounts

## Support Channels
- Email
- In-app chat
- Community support
- Ticketing
